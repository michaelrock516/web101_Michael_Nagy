Chicken Alfredo

This simple recipe is a family favorite. Using ready-made sauce, package noodles, garlic, and chicken, it is quick and easy to put together.

Ingredients

  • 3 jars Bertolli's Alfredo Sauce - Original
  • 1 lb pasta - I recommend Radiatori or Campenelle
  • 5 cloves (sections) of garlic - pressed
  • 2 or 3 chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Precook chicken - boil to shred or cut into bitesize pieces and stir fry until done. Set aside.
  2. In large pot - bring 6 cups water to boil. When water is boiling, add pasta. Cook as directed on package. Note: slightly undercooked (al dente) is better than overcooked.
  3. In another large pot combine chicken (shredded or chunks), 2 jars of sauce, and press in 5 cloves of fresh garlic.
  4. Heat on low heat.
  5. When pasta is done, drain and add to sauce.
  6. Stir and heat on low until ready to serve. Keep covered if not serving immediately so it won't dry out.

2 jars of sauce is probably sufficient for first serving, but if you like more sauce, add. After refrigerating, pasta will absorb more sauce so add reserved sauce or a little milk to make less dry.

Serve with side salad and/or toasted garlic bread.

Enjoy!

Ramen Broccoli Soup

A quick meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg chicken or beef Top Ramen
  • 1 cup broccoli flowerettes
  • 2 cups water

Steps

  1. Put water and broccoli in a sauce pan.
  2. When it comes to a boil, put in ramen noodles and seasoning.
  3. Turn off heat and let stand about 3 minutes.
  4. When noodles are to your liking, put in serving bowl.
  5. Grab a spoon.
  6. Be careful - this is hot!

Makes one big bowl (really two servings, but I'm not sharing!).

If you share, you may want to serve with side salad and/or toasted garlic bread.

Enjoy!

Honey Almond Cream Cheese

Get ready to enjoy a hot, toasted bagel with a sweet, nutty cream cheese!
My husband says, This is the best cream cheese ever!

Ingredients

  • 1 bagel - plain or your choice
  • 1 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh almonds (unsalted)
  • Honey to taste (about 2 TBS)

Steps

  1. In food processor, add cream cheese, almonds, and honey.
  2. Blend.
  3. Taste - add more nuts or honey if desired.
  4. Slice and toast bagel.
  5. Spread cream cheese on hot bagel.

Warm and yummy!.

Enjoy!
